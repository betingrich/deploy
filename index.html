Features of Mercedes Bot

  • Autostatusview: The bot will automatically view all the status updates of your contacts, saving you time and effort.
  • Music Video Downloader: Integrated with YouTube's API, this feature allows you to download music and videos directly to your device in high quality.
  • Access to ChatGPT: Use the bot to connect to premium ChatGPT servers, enabling AI-driven conversations and content generation.
  • Wallpaper Downloads: Get access to an extensive collection of stunning 4K wallpapers for your device, tailored to your preferences.
  • Image Downloads: With proper prompts, the bot fetches high-quality images from BingCloud and Google for your personal or professional use.
  • Logo Making: Generate professional-grade logos by providing the bot with a prompt or specific design requirements.
  • Anime Content: Search for and download anime videos and images directly through the bot with simple prompts.
  • Group Management: Manage your WhatsApp groups effectively by removing all members or enforcing rules like kicking users who share prohibited links.
  • Anti-Bad Words: Automatically detects and removes offensive words in group chats, keeping your groups clean and respectful.
  • Media Downloads: Download media from Facebook, TikTok, Twitter, MediaFire, and Google Drive links, often without watermarks.
  • Auto Sticker: Automatically converts images and short videos into fun stickers for WhatsApp chats when activated.
